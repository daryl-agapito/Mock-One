Who we are

Uniquely formulated medicine can make all the difference.

In certain instances, commercially available medication is less than a perfect fit for you. When this happens, healthcare professionals turn to a NABP-accredited compounding pharmacy like Customedico to create a solution. Compounding is the science of making medicine from scratch. This means the dose, formula, and form of the medicine can all be customized to the precise needs of each patient.

Customedico Pharmacy has been formulating and crafting compounded medications for our community since the 1970s. When your healthcare provider says you need a compounded medication, bring your prescription to Customedico and we’ll create your medicine to the highest standards of quality and consistency. The National Association of Boards of Pharmacy has created a set of strict standards which very few pharmacies are able to meet.
